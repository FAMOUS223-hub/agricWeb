Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a consultation with AgricWorld?

Starting a consultation is easy! Simply fill out the contact form on our contact page, or call our Accra office directly. Our experts will schedule an initial assessment of your needs within 48 hours.

Are your irrigation systems suitable for small-scale farms?

Absolutely. We design irrigation solutions for farms of all sizes. For small-scale operations, we often recommend our affordable solar-powered drip irrigation kits that maximize water efficiency at a low entry cost.

Do you provide organic certification support?

Yes, we guide our partners through the entire process of obtaining local and international organic certifications. We help with documentation, soil auditing, and ensuring your practices meet the required standards.
